Dine In Boston

About

Dine In Boston will be a website that is showcases the most popular and unique restaurants in Boston MA . From high end dining for those special occasions to the most affordable but delicious foods.

Objective

The goal of this website is to offer the following to the target audience:

  • Pick the top 3 or most popular menu items for each restaurant
  • Categorize restaurants by price, type of foods, and best hours to dine
  • Save time searching for restaurants and know exactly what to get
  • Show offers and promotions at restaurants i.e student discounts, free items

Target Audience

The target audience are people who eat out at least 1-2 times per week. Also, people who are traveling to or are new to the Boston area. This website mainly targets college student, travelers, and new residents of Boston.

Content

The homepage will showcase many of the restaurants, and then users can go to different pages based on the following categories: type of restaurant, price (low to high), and by offers and promotions. After the user picks a category, it will forward them to a list of restaurants under that category that they can choose from.
